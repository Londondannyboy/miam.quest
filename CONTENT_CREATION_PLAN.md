## Content Specifications

### MDX Template Structure

```mdx
## What is [Topic]?

**[Topic]** is... [First occurrence of keyword bolded]

[300-500 words introducing the topic]

## [H2 with Secondary Keyword]

[Content with internal links]

## [H2 with Related Keyword]

[Content with external authoritative links]

## Frequently Asked Questions

<FAQ question="[Question with keyword]?">
[Answer]
</FAQ>

<FAQ question="[Another question]?">
[Answer]
</FAQ>

## Next Steps

[CTA to speak with Miam or find mediator]
```

### Quality Requirements

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| Word Count | Min 1,500 words (supporting), 2,500+ words (pillar) |
| Keywords | Primary in H1, all H2s, bolded once, 5+ natural mentions |
| Internal Links | Min 5 per page |
| External Links | Min 3 authoritative sources (Gov.uk, FMC, HMCTS) |
| Schema | FAQPage or HowTo on every page |
| Key Facts | 2-4 fact cards where appropriate |

### Authoritative External Sources

| Source | URL | Use For |
